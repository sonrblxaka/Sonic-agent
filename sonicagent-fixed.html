How it works

Three steps to an agent that runs your stack

From setup to first automated action in under five minutes.

01

Connect your tools

Authorize your apps with one click. Sonic Agent handles OAuth, tokens, and permissions — you just pick what to connect.

FAQ

Questions we actually get

No fluff. If your question isn't here, reach us through the chat widget below.

No plain-text secrets are ever stored. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest with AES-256 and scoped to the minimum permissions your agent needs. You can revoke access for any integration at any time from your dashboard.

Every action is logged with a full input/output trace. You can replay, undo, or roll back most actions directly from the live monitoring dashboard. For irreversible operations (like sending emails or deleting data), you can configure a confirmation gate before the agent proceeds.

The Starter plan supports 1 active agent, Pro supports 10, and Enterprise is unlimited. "Active" means currently running a workflow — you can have as many agents defined as you like on any plan.

A self-hosted open-core option is planned for 2027. Enterprise customers on the current cloud plan can already choose their data residency region (US, EU, or APAC) and opt into VPC isolation.
